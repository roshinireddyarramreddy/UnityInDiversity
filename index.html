About India

Land of Diversity

The diversities in India is unique. Being a large country with large population , India represents endless varieties of physical features and cultural patterns. It is the land of many languages. It is only in India People professes all major religions in the world.

There are close to 1600 languages spoken in India. India also has geographic diversity in the form of mountains, plains, plateaus, deserts and islands.

India is called the land of diversity because, we have various types of food, speak different languages, celebrate different festivals, and practise different religions and traditions.

Hyderabad

A great mix of Cultures

Hyderabad is one of the largest cities in India. Settled in the 16th century, it was a pearl and diamond trading center. Today, it's a hub for IT services.

This city is a mix of languages, cutures, and religions, which results in many holiday festivals. Public parks and botanical gardens are the main choice for leisure times and offer th perfect spaces for you to play and relax.

Banglore

Tech capital of India

Banglore is the capital of the Karnataka state in Southern India, and is one of the country's largest cities. It became a center for the high-tech industry in the 20th century and it is known as the 'Silicon Valley of India'

The city offers you a vibrant culture scene. On top of a thriving film industry, there are theaters, music, and pubs. It's also a major center for Indian classical music and dance festivals all year around.

Delhi

A vibrant blend of tradition and modernity

Capital of empires for thousands of years, Delhi remains a commercial, cultural, and political centre.

The city's two distinct areas- Old and New Delhi- represent its ties to history and culture, as well as it's modern presence on a global stage. Monuments and places of worship co-exist with leisure centres, restaurants , and sports venues.

Chennai

The Cultural Capital of India

Chennai is the capital of Tamil Nadu state. Located along the Bay of Bengal, it's known as the Gateway to Southern India. Since the 1990s, the tech sector has been a major contributor to the local economy.

This city is a major cultural center. Every year, there are music and dance festivals with hundreds of artists. Chennai features vibrant theatre and cinema scenes, museums, beautiful parks, and green spaces dotted across the city.
